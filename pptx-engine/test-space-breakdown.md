# Space Breakdown Test Prompts

Sample natural language prompts to paste into the space breakdown field on the frontend.

## Basic Office

```
18 open workstations, 2 private offices, 1 large conference room, and 2 phone booths
```

## Mid-Size Office

```
30 open workstations, 4 private offices, 2 executive offices, 1 large conference room, 2 small conference rooms, 3 huddle rooms, 6 phone booths, 1 reception area, 1 break room, and a lounge
```

## Minimal Setup

```
10 open desks, 1 conference room, and 1 phone booth
```

## Training-Focused

```
15 open workstations, 1 training room for 40 people, 2 huddle rooms, 1 reception, and 3 phone booths
```

## Executive Suite

```
3 executive offices, 5 private offices, 1 large conference room for 20 people, 2 medium conference rooms, 1 reception area, 1 lounge, and 1 break room
```

## Supported Space Types

| Space Type | Example Phrases |
|---|---|
| `open_workstation` | open workstations, open desks |
| `private_office` | private offices |
| `executive_office` | executive offices |
| `conference_room` | conference room (small/medium/large) |
| `huddle_room` | huddle rooms |
| `phone_booth` | phone booths |
| `training_room` | training room |
| `reception` | reception area |
| `lounge` | lounge |
| `break_room` | break room |
